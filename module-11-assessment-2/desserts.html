Malva Pudding

Malva Pudding

My nan was telling me fond memories of her childhood and an aunt who has since moved to canada made her this. She couldn’t contact said aunt or find a recipe. I made her this for her birthday and it bought tears to her eyes..

I tried this again yesterday and baked for an hour til it was golden brown. I turned the oven off and left it in there. As a result there wasn't any custard left, but it tasted a lot better than last time! Next time i'll take it out of the oven once it's done, even if we don't eat it straightaway.

Perfect winter fare - easy, quick and so tasty.

This pudding turned out perfectly and looked good. Flavourwise it was a bit too eggy for me, this could be because I reduced the sugar. Next time I will use the full quantity of sugar..

An easy throw-together dessert that forms a crust, custard and topping while in the oven. Easy.
